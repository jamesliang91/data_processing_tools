Momentum Phenomenon and trading Strategy on Chinese stock market
Author: Minghan (James) Liang, Jun.2021-Aug.2021

1. This research is primarily focus on the price momentum phenomenon on stock assets in chinese stock market, as well as feasible 
trading strategies over any statistically significant market phenomenons related to the price momentum phenomenons.
